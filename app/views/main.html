-Depression is a state of mind where individual has
-Low mood most part of the day and almost every day.
-Diminished interest in previously pleasurable activities and unable to enjoy.
-Decreased energy leading to easy tiring for small efforts and unable to work. Along with these individuals also have -Poor concentration affecting daily routine working and forgetting things.
-Low at self
-confidence where individual feels that he/she wonâ€™t be able to achieve anything though in the past he/she was an achiever.
-Feeling guilty without any mistake and excessive blaming himself/herself without any reason.
-Feeling worthless of living.
-Wishing for death, self
-harming behaviour and attempting suicides.
-Increased or decreased sleep.
-Increased or decreased appetite (Weight loss or Weight gain > 5%).

THE SYMPTOMS SHOULD BE PERSISTENT FOR MORE THAN 2 WEEKS

2) WHO ARE AT RISK OF DEPRESSION?


Depression results from a complex interaction of social, psychological and Biological factors. People who have gone through adverse life events (unemployment, bereavement, psychological trauma) are more likely to develop depression.
-Stressful life events (Love Failure, Death in Family, Work stress).
-Living Alone.
-Marital Issues.
-Financial Stress.
-Someone in family having depression.
-Childhood bad experiences (Abusive Father, Single Parent).

3) WHAT TO DO TO GET AWAY FROM DEPRESSION?


-Talk to someone close, avoid staying alone.
-Keep your daily routine same.
-Keep your day busy.
-Try sleeping for 8hrs.
-Pick up an old hobby and start again.
-Exercise daily or yoga and meditation helps you to improve symptoms.
-Avoid food with high sugars and keep away from alcohol and other drugs.
-Challenge your negative thoughts.

4) WHEN TO SEE A DOCTOR?


-When the symptoms are severe enough which are disturbing your Social, Occupational and Personal life.
-Avoid stigma on depression. It is curable once you approach for help.
-As you talk about your fracture or any surgery feel free to talk about your state of mind, after all mind is also part of you.
